# 📝 Content Guidelines

## 🌟 Clue Progression Strategy
To maintain difficulty curves, clues must follow a strict level of specificity:

```
[Clue 1: Extremely Broad]
         ↓
[Clue 2: Broad Category]
         ↓
[Clue 3: Contextual / Medium Hint]
         ↓
[Clue 4: Specific Detail]
         ↓
[Clue 5: Almost Revealing]
```

---

## 🚫 Restrictive Content Policies
Puzzles must be free of political statements, religious views, and offensive references to ensure inclusive daily play.
